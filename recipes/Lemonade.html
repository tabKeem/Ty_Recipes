Lemonade

Home

Description

Yes, you will use the entire lemon for this whole lemon lemonade recipe. Summer has never tasted sweeter.

Ingredients

  • 2 organic lemons, quartered and seeded
  • 6 cups cold water, divided
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups ice

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine lemons, sugar, and 4 cups water in a blender and process until completely smooth, 1 to 2 minutes.
  3. Pour lemonade through a fine mesh strainer into a pitcher and discard solids.
  4. Stir in ice and remaining 2 cups of water.
